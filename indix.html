Welcome to Saudi Arabia!

Discover the enchanting beauty of Saudi Arabia, a land rich in history, culture, and natural wonders. From the sacred cities of Mecca and Medina to the stunning landscapes of AlUla, each region offers a unique experience.

We invite you to explore the vibrant cities, indulge in delicious cuisine, and immerse yourself in the warm hospitality of the Saudi people.

Your favorite country to visit: Saudi Arabia

Attractions

Mecca: The holiest city in Islam, home to the Kaaba and the Grand Mosque.

Medina: The second holiest city, where the Prophet's Mosque is located.

AlUla: A UNESCO World Heritage site known for its rock formations and archaeological sites.

Riyadh: The capital city, featuring modern architecture and cultural sites like the National Museum.

Activities

Enjoy a variety of activities across the Kingdom:

  • Desert safaris in the Empty Quarter.
  • Cultural events and festivals throughout the year.
  • Shopping in traditional markets and modern malls.
  • Exploring the stunning Red Sea coastline.
